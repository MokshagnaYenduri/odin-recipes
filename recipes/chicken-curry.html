Chicken curry

This Indian-inspired creamy chicken curry recipe is similar to a curry I had in India. The aromatic spices and flavors are a delight to the senses! Delicious with fresh naan and basmati rice.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breast or thighs
  • 2 medium onions, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the chicken and cook
  2. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside. Add the onions to the skillet
  3. Add the garlic and ginger to the skillet and cook for 1 minute
  4. Add the cumin, curry powder, turmeric, and cayenne pepper
  5. Add the diced tomatoes, chicken broth, and tomato paste to the skillet
  6. Return the chicken to the skillet and stir to coat with the curry sauce
  7. Reduce heat to low and simmer, covered, for 20-25 minutes
  8. Stir in the heavy cream and cook for an additional 5 minutes
