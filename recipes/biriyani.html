Biriyani

Biriyani, a spicy chicken and rice dish, is one of the most well-known Indian-Pakistani dishes.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 1 cup chicken breast or thighs
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add cumin seeds and let them sizzle for a few seconds.
  3. Add onion and garlic and saut√© until the onion is translucent.
  4. Add chicken and cook until it is browned and cooked through.
  5. Add rice, coriander powder, garam masala powder, salt, and other spices
  6. Add lemon juice and mix well.
  7. Transfer the mixture to a serving dish and garnish with cilantro.
